Step-by-step setup
1

Power on your DueLight

Plug the device into any USB power source (phone charger, computer, etc.). The LED will blink while it starts up. After a few seconds it will enter setup mode.

2

Connect to the DueLight Wi-Fi

On your phone or computer, open Wi-Fi settings and connect to:
Network: Canvas_LED_Tracker Password: canvas123
Your device may say "No Internet" — that's normal. Stay connected.

3

Open the setup page

Open a browser and go to http://192.168.4.1
You should see the DueLight setup screen. If it doesn't load, make sure you're still on the Canvas_LED_Tracker network.

4

Enter your home Wi-Fi and Canvas token

Fill in your home Wi-Fi name and password. Then get your Canvas API token (the student should do this):

  1. Open Canvas and log in as the student
  2. Click Account (top-left) → Settings
  3. Scroll to Approved Integrations → click + New Access Token
  4. Description: DueLight
  5. Leave the expiry date blank (or set as far ahead as allowed)
  6. Click Generate Token, then copy and paste it into the setup page
5

Set timezone and save

Choose your timezone from the dropdown (or leave it as US Eastern if you're on the East Coast). Hit Save Settings. The device will reboot, connect to your Wi-Fi, and start tracking assignments automatically.

What do the lights mean?
Solid green — No urgent assignments. You're good!
Pulsing yellow — Assignment due within your yellow warning window.
Pulsing red — Assignment due very soon (within your red warning window).
Red blinking slowly (2 blinks, pause) — Wi-Fi disconnected. Check your router.
Red blinking rapidly — Canvas token expired. See below to renew.
Renewing your Canvas token
Canvas tokens expire after up to 4 months. Your DueLight will warn you ahead of time — when it does, follow these steps.
  1. Log into Canvas as the student
  2. Go to Account → Settings
  3. Scroll to Approved Integrations → click + New Access Token
  4. Description: DueLight — leave expiry blank
  5. Click Generate Token and copy it
  6. Connect to your home Wi-Fi, open http://duelight.local or http://192.168.4.1 (if in setup mode), paste the new token, and save
Frequently asked questions
The setup page won't load at 192.168.4.1

Make sure your phone/computer is connected to Canvas_LED_Tracker Wi-Fi. Some devices will automatically switch back to your home network if they detect no internet — you may need to disable "Auto-switch" or "Smart Network Switch" in your Wi-Fi settings.

The Wi-Fi test says it failed but my password is right

Double-check for typos — passwords are case-sensitive. Also make sure your router is 2.4 GHz; the ESP32 does not support 5 GHz networks.

Canvas "Test" says it failed

Make sure the token was copied fully (it's quite long). Also confirm you're logging into Canvas as the student, not a parent account. If the issue persists, delete the token in Canvas Settings and generate a new one.

The light is green but I know there are assignments due

The DueLight only tracks items on your Canvas To Do list that haven't been submitted yet. If a teacher hasn't published the assignment yet, or it was submitted through a third-party extension, it may not appear. You can also check the threshold settings at http://duelight.local.

Can I adjust when it turns yellow or red?

Yes! Connect to your home Wi-Fi and open http://duelight.local in a browser. The Settings page lets you set the yellow and red thresholds (in days/hours), as well as quiet hours when the light stays off.

The device isn't connecting to my Wi-Fi after setup

Hold the reset button for 5 seconds to re-enter setup mode, then go through setup again. Make sure you selected the correct network and that the password doesn't include unusual special characters.
